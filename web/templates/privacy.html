Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-03-16

This Privacy Policy explains how Gamma Chatbot collects, uses, stores, and deletes personal data from users interacting through web and WhatsApp channels.

1. Data We Collect

  • Messages sent to the chatbot and generated responses.
  • Basic account information for authenticated web users (for example, username).
  • WhatsApp metadata required to reply to your messages (for example, phone number).

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide chatbot responses and improve service quality.
  • To secure and monitor the service.
  • To comply with legal obligations where applicable.

3. Data Retention

We retain chat records and account data only as long as required for operational or legal purposes, or until a valid deletion request is completed.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal data. We may process data through trusted providers required to operate the service (for example hosting, database, and WhatsApp Cloud API providers).

5. Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data. To request deletion, use the Data Deletion Request page.

6. Contact

For privacy inquiries, contact the service administrator through the support channel listed in your deployment.
